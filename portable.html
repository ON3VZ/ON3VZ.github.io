Section 3

11m Citizen Band · 27 MHz

No licence needed. A local POTA-inspired family radio network for the Ardennes holiday house.

On family holidays in the Ardennes, the plan is to build a local CB radio network using 27 MHz (Citizen Band). No amateur radio licence required — fully legal in Belgium and the Netherlands with CE-marked equipment. The full family can participate from day one.

The setup combines the two PNI HP 82 handhelds already owned (4W FM) with a proper base station (Albrecht AE 5900 Mini SSB) and a mobile car station — giving 12W SSB range of 20–60 km vs the 2–8 km of the handhelds. The holiday house becomes the radio hub.
