2019
Where it all began — radio waves & the sea
A lifelong interest in radio waves, sparked playfully on holiday with PMR446 handhelds. After earning my sailing licence and RYA Dayskipper diploma, I needed a maritime radio operator certificate. Obtained my VHF licence, then my SRC certificate. Though the focus was on procedures and equipment, my first contact with BIPT was made — and the curiosity about radio only grew stronger.
